Transport Research Arena (TRA)

I will be participating in the Transport Research Arena 2024 (TRA2024), the largest European research and innovation conference on transport and mobility. I will be presenting our research titled “Improving the planning of future track interventions using digital tools”, which showcases the a part of STABILITY research project. The paper demonstrates how digital tools are to be used for improving the planning of future track interventions.

I am looking forward to meeting experts from academia and industry and exchanging ideas. I also look forward to closely get to know the latest developments and innovations in the transport sector, as well as expanding my network and exploring new collaboration opportunities.

I hope to see you in Dublin!
